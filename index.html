Welcome to favPoke - Favorite Pokemon picker!

Keep choosing your favorite Pokemon until you discover your absolute best. You can filter them by their native region or type if you like, or just play the game with every pokemon ever discovered!

How it Works

Welcome to favPoke! Here's how you can use the app:

  • Click "Start the Game" to begin discovering your favorite Pokémon.
  • Choose a path: "All Pokémon," "By Region," or "By Type."
  • Select your favorite Pokémon from the displayed options.
  • Continue selecting until only one Pokémon remains – your favorite!
  • Save your favorite Pokémon to the list for future reference.
