Welcome to favMon - Favorite Pokémon picker!

Find out what your favorite Pokémon is. Decide between the given Pokémon until only your favorite of them all remains!

How it Works

Welcome to favMon! Here's how you can use the app:

  • Click "Find your favorite" to begin.
  • Choose one of the options: "Random 60", "By Region", "By Type." or "All Pokémon". Each option will display the respective Pokémon. Random 60 will display sixty Pokémon at random.
  • Select your favorite Pokémon from the displayed options. After going through all Pokémon, the game will display the Pokémon you picked
  • Continue selecting until only one Pokémon remains – your favorite!
